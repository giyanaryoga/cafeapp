about us

what is Gya?

“Gya” actually referred to old Javanese language that means “rapid, tactical, quick”. Value that relates to GYA determined as dynamic movement that always keeping up the flows progressively.

GYA is not only a meeting point during lunch, but also a creative space that provides you a suitable working space & art installation for artisans.It rises as our concern to Yogyakarta creative industry. We believe through collaborative values, we will be able to grow together.

This is a wide space where people will be able to express the freedom and speak the urbane out of their wisdom.
